Sure! Here's a quick recipe for "Fennel and Orange Salad with Yogurt Dressing":

Ingredients:

- 1 medium fennel bulb, trimmed and thinly sliced
- 2 medium oranges, peeled and sliced
- 1/2 cup Greek yogurt
- 2 tbsp. extra-virgin olive oil
- 1 tbsp. honey
- 1 tbsp. freshly squeezed orange juice
- Salt and black pepper to taste

Instructions:

1. In a large bowl, combine the sliced fennel and orange slices.

2. In a small mixing bowl, whisk together the Greek yogurt, olive oil, honey, and orange juice.

3. Season the dressing with salt and black pepper to taste.

4. Drizzle the dressing over the fennel and orange mixture and toss gently to coat.

5. Serve the salad immediately, garnished with a few sprigs of fennel fronds.

Note: You may also want to buy some additional vegetables or ingredients like mixed greens, nuts, or seeds to add to the salad or serve it as a side dish with a meal.
