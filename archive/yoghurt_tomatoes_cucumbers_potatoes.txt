Greek-style Potato Salad with Yogurt Dressing

Note: You'll also need olive oil, red onion, garlic, lemon, salt, pepper, and fresh parsley.

Ingredients:
- 1 1/2 lbs. potatoes, peeled and cubed
- 3 tbsp. olive oil
- 1/2 red onion, thinly sliced
- 2 garlic cloves, minced
- 1 cup cherry tomatoes, halved
- 1 cup cucumber, chopped
- 1 cup Greek yogurt
- 1 tbsp. lemon juice
- Salt and pepper to taste
- 2 tbsp. chopped fresh parsley

Instructions:
1. Preheat the oven to 425°F. Arrange the cubed potatoes on a baking sheet and drizzle with 2 tbsp. of olive oil. Season with salt and pepper and toss to coat.
2. Roast the potatoes in the oven for 20-25 minutes or until tender and golden brown. Remove from the oven and set aside.
3. Heat the remaining 1 tbsp. of olive oil in a skillet over medium heat. Add the sliced red onion and minced garlic and sauté for 2-3 minutes or until softened.
4. Add the cherry tomatoes and chopped cucumber to the skillet and cook for another 2-3 minutes or until the vegetables are just tender. Remove from heat and set aside.
5. In a bowl, whisk together the Greek yogurt, lemon juice, salt, and pepper to make the dressing.
6. Combine the roasted potatoes and vegetable mixture in a large bowl. Pour the dressing over the vegetables and toss to coat.
7. Garnish the potato salad with chopped parsley and serve at room temperature.

Note: You will also need to buy some extra olive oil, red onion, garlic, lemon, salt, pepper, and fresh parsley for this recipe.
